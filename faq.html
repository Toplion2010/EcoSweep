Frequently Asked Questions

What is EcoSweep?
EcoSweep is a company that creates a robot that can clean beaches.
What is the goal of your project?
Our goal is to create a robot for cleaning garbage on the beach - to automate the cleaning process to protect the environment. This helps to reduce pollution, keep beaches clean, improve the ecological situation and increase tourist attractiveness.
